OVERVIEW

PROBLEM SPACE

​QB(QQ Browser, which has largest monthly active users among Chinese web browsers) has diverse user group and users’ motivations include both entertainment and working. So QB keeps rolling out new functionalities like providing great movie or reading e-books experience.

​But when all of these put together, will users confuse about it?

​A common solution for accurate recommendation is personalization. A lot of products already provide personalizing news, feeds, or activities, but how about personalizing UI? That’s when I initiated an idea of personalizing UI using machine learning, and recommending more accurate functionalities to users.

UNDERSTADNDING

RESEARCH GOAL

QB keeps iterating and rolling out new functionalities. The research aims to understand users’ actual perceptions and word of mouth. By comparison of customer satisfaction with competitive products, we can narrow down problems.

How can functionalities affect users’ satisfaction and willingness to recommend(NPS)?

ONLINE SURVEY 1

To understand users’ satisfaction of different functionalities, we distributed survey to QB active users and got 745 responses:

Function with higher customer satisfaction:
- Easy to use functions (3.86/5)
- Interface effect (3.83/5)
- Interface Layout (3.8/5)
- Brand image (3.8/5)

Function with lower customer satisfaction:
- Occupation of system resources (2.94/5)
- Advertisement (3.02/5)


We also collected users’ feedback from the survey. Feedback related to functionalities were concentrated on advertisement(pop-up window) strategy :
- “Too much advertisement!”
- “Always unnecessary pop-up windows.”
- “It recommends useless video, shopping, etc. Just a waste of time.”
- “The starting pages are full of advertisements. I even cannot close them.”
- …

ONLINE SURVEY 2

To compare users’ satisfaction with existing products on the market, we also distributed survey to QQ marketing users and got results from 662 users, whose default browsers are Chrome(139), 360 Browser(302), QB(221) respectively:

- Compared with 360 Browser, QB are weaker in occupation of system resources, complete functions.
- Compared with Chrome Browser, QB are especially weaker in advertisement.
- Compared with Chrome Browser, QB are better in content acquisition.

CHALLENGE 2: CUSTOMIZATION VS. EFFICIENCY

For “Do Not Disturb” mode, we provide users auto on/off at working time. Ideally, two time slots should be provided(morning and afternoon) and all provide customization. But is it too much for them to start? How to balance between complete customization and easy interaction?


Solution 1: Complete Customization. Cons: Users may confuse why these two time slots.

Solution 2: Pros: More direct to tell users what the break time is for(lunch break). Cons: Does users need to customize so specifically at the first time?

Solution 3: Quick start and customization later - First time, users just need to select to auto open on office hour or not(hover will show specific time), and they can customize it later at toolbar.
